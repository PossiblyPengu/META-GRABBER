    How it works

    1. Drop your MP3 chapters in playback order.
    2. Fine-tune order or remove tracks if needed.
    3. Click “Compile to M4B” to merge & convert.
    4. Download the finished .m4b file once the process completes. We discard uploads immediately after conversion.

    Heads up: conversion runs locally on this server and requires FFmpeg to be installed.
